Project Overview

This web application will be designed to be a social app, with the objective of providing help, resources and closure to women suffering from the reproductive health issue mentioned above. The application will enable users to speak and chat with a licensed medical practitioner. It will also create opportunities for creators to share life hacks and story. The application will give the user the ability to interact with other users, access shared resources and house closed community operated by these same users.
