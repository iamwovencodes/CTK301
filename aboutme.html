Oku Emmanuella is a second year graduate student at Illinois State University pursing a master degree in Creative Technologies. She is an alumna of Zenith University College, Accra Ghana and University of Lagos, Nigeria.

She is passionate about sharing her knowledge of UI/UX design to beginners, developing social media strategy, teaching and most recently knitting. In her spare time she is either listening to a podcast, her curated YouTube playlist or volunteering as a teaching instructor. Here creative interest lies within the realms of digital media and user experience research.
